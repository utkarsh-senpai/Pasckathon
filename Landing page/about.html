About WorkShare

WorkShare is a platform where any individual can delegate their own projects or work in another. You don't need any prior experience to join us. A hands-on experience is the best way to learn, we are here to kick-start your journey in various domains, for you to explore them, and give back something productive to the community. You can create your own project and assign tasks, with suitable deadlines, to your project applicants. Also, you can work on other’s projects! It’s always a win-win situation for both sides. Looking forward for you to work on exciting projets!
